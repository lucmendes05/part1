part1

before you close those curtains,
before you flick that light off,
do not forget to be reminded by
the trail you leave behind;

fearfully and wonderfully made,
the stars bend down to greet you,
in a moment, a closed eye,
aligned with each dimension,
rocked gently away;

never more comfortable to be quiet,
still, and silent,
the curls of smoke rise to frame your face,
you slip away to some secluded place;

i think that i will be travelling for a lifetime,
changing the path, the future for the benefit of the past,
seeing doors that open and close and fall
under the weight of things i could not amend,

please do not be too tired to breathe,
the squares will blend, spill over
hidden in reflections of his analyses,
may you never be alone;

when you open those curtains,
just as you flick that light on,
do not forget to be reminded by
the day you have ahead.
